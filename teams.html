Cipher's Best Teams



Cipher F2P Team (Free to Play)

DPS DPS Support Healer
Cipher

Any DPS 		Asta Lynx

For a free-to-play team, you can use any DPS with Cipher provided that they benefit from her debuffs. As for other units, Asta is a great F2P Support for Cipher since she can buff her SPD, which Cipher needs.



Cipher Acheron Team

DPS DPS Support Healer
Cipher Acheron Jaioqiu Aventurine

Cipher can do great in an Acheron team as both a Sub-DPS and a speedy debuffer that can help buildup Slashed Dream. With E0 Acheron, you will need another Nihility character to unlock the full damage buff.
If Acheron is at E2, you can use a Harmony unit since she only needs one Nihility unit to activate the damage buff fully.

Alternative Explanation
Silver Wolf Support Replacement :
Silver Wolf is best used against fewer Elite enemies and can be SP-friendly.
Pela Support Replacement :
Pela is a 4-star Nihility unit who can provide DEF shred with her Ultimate.
Sunday Support Replacement :
Best used if Acheron is E2. While the energy restoration is negligible, Acheron can benefit from the CRIT buffs.
Tribbie Support Replacement :
Best used if Acheron is E2. Tribbie can buff both Cipher and Acheron and help deal more damage to Elite enemies.
Sparkle Support Replacement :
Best used if Acheron is E2. Sparkle can provide a DMG boost and more Skill Points for Acheron.
Guinaifen Support Replacement :
Guinaifen is a 4-star Nihility unit who can provide debuffs with Burn DoTs and Firekiss
Gallagher Sustain Replacement :
Gallagher is a healer that can provide debuffs with his Ultimate and Enhanced Basic Attack.
Anyone from Abundance, or Preservation Sustain Replacement :
Anyone who can keep you alive will work fine.



Cipher Follow-Up Team

DPS DPS Support Healer
Cipher Feixiao Robin Aventurine

As a Follow-Up attacker, Cipher can be an excellent Sub-DPS to Feixiao or Dr. Ratio thanks to her frequent attacks and debuffs. Ideally, you'd want units that can provide debuffs to have the Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters at full uptime.



Alternative Explanation
Dr. Ratio DPS Replacement :
Dr. Ratio benefits from Cipher's debuffs and her Follow-up attacks. The more debuffs an enemy has, the more likely Dr. Ratio can unleash a Follow-up attack.
Tribbie Support Replacement :
Tribbie can be used with Feixiao since her Follow-up attacks can help stack Flying Aureus.
Jaioqiu Support Replacement :
Jiaoqiu increases Ultimate DMG and DMG taken by enemies, which can greatly increase Feixiao's overall damage.
Ruan Mei Support Replacement :
Ruan Mei provides a lot of offensive buffs and extends enemies' Weakness Broken state through her Ultimate.
Pela Support Replacement :
Pela reduces the DEF of enemies with her Ultimate which allows Feixiao to deal more damage against them.
Hyacine Sustain Replacement :
Hyacine can attack frequently with Little Ica and provide party-wide healing.
Anyone from Abundance, or Preservation Sustain Replacement :
Anyone who can keep Feixiao and the team will work fine except Houhou since Feixiao can't benefit from the Energy Regen.

Cipher's Kit



Cipher's Abbilities and Skills


Talent Inflicts Patron on The Enemy

Cipher's Talent allows her to inflict the Patron state on the enemy with the highest Max HP. When the enemy gets attacked by her allies, Cipher records the damage dealt to the enemy, and she performs Follow-Up Attacks based on it.


Ultimate Deals True DMG

Cipher's Ultimate deals True DMG based on the damage recorded by her Talent.


Skill Changes the Target of Patron

Cipher's Skill changes the target of the Patron state. The damage recorded will be retained even when the target of the Patron state is changed.


Technique Increases Movespeed

Cipher's Technique increases her movespeed and gives her invisibility, which lets her become undetectable by enemies.



Cipher's Trace Priority



Combat Traces

Trace Priority + Explanation

Basic Attack 		Medium Priority :
Cipher's Basic Attack should be saved for last since you'd want to use Cipher's Skill more.

Skill 		High Priority :
More times than not, you will be using Cipher's Skill, so upgrade this as soon as you've maxed out her Ultimate and Talent.

Ultimate 		Highest Priority :
Combined with the True DMG multiplier, Cipher's Ultimate can deal high damage.

Talent 		Highest Priority :
With Cipher's high SPD stat, you'd want to level Cipher's Talent for optimal off-turn damage through her Follow-Up Attacks



Bonus Traces

Trace Priority + Explanation

Sleight of Sky 		Highest Priority :
Cipher's Follow-Up Attacks deal 100% more CRIT DMG and all enemies receive increased DMG when she's active.

300 Rogues 		Highest Priority :
Cipher gains tally through allies attacking enemies other than the Patron.

Empyrean Strides 		Highest Priority :
This unlocks Cipher's CRIT Rate buff by simply having a high enough SPD stat. This will also increase the gained tally.
